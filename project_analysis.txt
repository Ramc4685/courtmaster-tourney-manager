# CourtMaster - Revolutionizing Indoor Sports Tournament Management

## Why CourtMaster?

Tournament organization in indoor sports, particularly badminton, has long been plagued by manual processes and disconnected systems. CourtMaster aims to revolutionize this space by providing an integrated solution that automates the most time-consuming aspects of tournament management.

## Core Pain Points We're Solving

### For Tournament Organizers
1. **Complex Bracket Management**
   - Currently: Manual creation and updates, prone to errors
   - Our Solution: Automated bracket generation with intelligent seeding
   - Benefit: Saves hours of planning and reduces errors

2. **Court Assignment Challenges**
   - Currently: Manual coordination, inefficient court usage
   - Our Solution: Automated court assignment system
   - Benefit: Maximizes court utilization and streamlines tournament flow

3. **Division Management**
   - Currently: Difficult to manage multiple skill levels
   - Our Solution: Automated group stages with division-specific rules
   - Benefit: Ensures fair play across all skill levels

4. **Referee Coordination**
   - Currently: Manual scheduling and assignment
   - Our Solution: Automated referee assignment with digital scoring
   - Benefit: Ensures proper coverage and reduces coordination overhead

### For Players
1. **Match Timing Uncertainty**
   - Currently: Unclear when next matches will start
   - Our Solution: Real-time updates and notifications
   - Benefit: Players can better prepare for their matches

2. **Communication Gaps**
   - Currently: Limited updates about tournament progress
   - Our Solution: Integrated communication system
   - Benefit: Players stay informed about schedule changes
